What is Starsector?

Starsector is a single player Sci-fi themed RPG game with sandbox element and its emphasis on space-combat, roleplaying, exploration, and in-game economy. The game put you on the role of a newly inducted space captain who is seeking fortune and glory in amidst the vast and lawless expanse of Perseus Sector.

The story so far...

Humanity has reached its peak technological advancement. One of the greatest human achievements was the birth of AI. With it, they created the most sophisticated civilization to ever exist, built spaceships to brave the uncharted space, and finalized their mega-project, the Gate Network, that could carry them to the outmost fringes of the known galaxy. As it seems, there is nothing that can stop Humanity from conquering the whole galaxy. Until there is...

And just like that...

The Gate Network that connected every human colony in the whole galaxy automatically ceases to operate. As for the cause, there is neither an accountable witness nor proof that can shed light on what is known to be the cause of “The Collapse”. Colonies that were unlucky enough to colonize themselves far from the Persean Sector soon after the sudden dissolution of the Gate Network have been cut off from any communication or transport. Which causes the colony to fall into disarray and later plunge into darkness. Those who are in the Persean sector haven’t seen the worst yet.

Post Collapse Period

As the once-united colony under the Domain banner cut themselves off and created their own faction, not long after, due to the rising tension in the sector, the corporation deployed their AI war fleets to assume dominance over the Persean sector, setting the stage for the first and second AI wars. This event will last for a hundred cycles. With colonies being decimated by constant orbital bombardments, technology was lost to the fires of war, and once formidable fleets were reduced to a fraction of their initial strength. After what seems forever, a cease-fire treaty between all factions is signed, effectively ending the war. As for the technology and infrastructure lost during the war, nothing could ever bring them back.

What is your role in the present event?

The current cycle is C+206. The scars left from the first and second AI wars still burn brightly inside the Persean sector. And the threat of another intersystem conflict lingers. You take on the role of a newly inducted space captain with your small fleets of frigates and small cargo ships. What will you become, captain? Will you become a merchant and earn your living through honest trading? Will you find the most sophisticated piece of lost technology and become the richest space captain in the whole galaxy? Live your life by marauding, raiding, and smuggling as a space pirate? Or settle in a paradise world where you create your space-conquering empire? All roads lead to Askonia, and your path will determine the shape of the whole sector. Now turn on your hyper-generator and carve your path in this lawless galaxy. May your stars shine brighter, captain.

What can you find in Starsector?

  • Command your fleet in tactical battles
